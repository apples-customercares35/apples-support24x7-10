What Detailing Package to Choose

Full Service Car Detailing Locations

When choosing automotive services at Super Star Car Wash, our menu can seem overwhelming with all of the options available. We provide various services at our 4 Arizona Super Star Car Wash Quick Lube shops locations from automotive detaling to changing most fluids in your car, light bulbs, filters… the list goes on.

Carnauba Hand Wax

For our carnauba hand wax treatment at Super Star Car Wash, we apply Meguiar’s carnauba wax to your car’s paint. This gives it protection from small debris and sun damage. Not only does it protect your car’s paint job, but the paint will have a glossy shine to it!

Complete Interior Detail

With a complete interior detail, we'll shampoo your vehicle's carpet, clean the seats, dash, doors, and console, and apply a protective treatment from Meguiar's. The inside of your vehicle will look as great as the outside.

Carpet Shampoo

Carpet shampooing service is a process of cleaning the carpets in a vehicle, it removes dirt, crumbs, and other debris. This service at Super Star Car Wash goes deep, using a shampoo to clean the carpets, leaving them looking and feeling refreshed.

Seat & Upholstery Cleaning

Seat and Upholstery cleaning service is a process that uses a deep shampoo to remove dirt, crumbs and other debris, leaving the seats and upholstery looking and feeling refreshed.

Super Clean

Super Clean service is a comprehensive cleaning service for the interior of a vehicle, it includes cleaning the dash, doors, and console. Super Star Car Wash also offers an additional service of applying leather conditioner to the leather surfaces of the vehicle to give an extra level of protection and shine.

Rim Polish

Rim polish service is a process of cleaning and polishing the rims of a vehicle, it removes loose dirt, dust and other debris, also adding a shine to the rims.

Headlight Restoration

Headlight restoration service refreshes yellowed and fogged-over headlights. The service at Super Star Car Wash is designed to clear up the headlights, making them clearer and brighter, improving visibility and safety while driving at night.

Ultimate Detail + Simoniz Diamond Plate

Two Step Buff & Wax, Clay Bar Mitt Treatment, Complete Interior Detail, Headlight Restoration, 2 Year Warranty on Diamond Plate

Mini Detail

At Super Star Car Wash, customers have the option to choose any two items from the Carpet Shampoo, Seat & Upholstery Shampoo, Interior Clean (Dash, Doors, Console) services to customize their cleaning package and suit their specific needs.

Diamond Plate

Adds a second layer of protection, including Clay Mitt, Buff & Wax.

Two Step Buff & Wax

Removes light scratches and oxidation.

Clay Magic

Removes overspray, bug residue, and tree sap.

Paint Sealant

Gives you an extra layer of protection.

Orbital Polish

Provides up to 6 months of protection, including Carnauba Wax Treatment.

Complete Detail Package

If you want your car to look as close to new as possible inside and out, get Super Star Car Wash’s complete detail package. It includes everything in the complete interior detail, along with buffing and waxing the exterior, a paint sealant applied for an extra layer of protection, and headlight restoration.
